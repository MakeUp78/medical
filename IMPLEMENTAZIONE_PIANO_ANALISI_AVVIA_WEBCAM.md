# Implementazione Piano Analisi Avvia Webcam

## Action Plan

### Identified Issues
1. **Low Resolution of Webcam**  
   - Many users reported that the webcam resolution is not satisfactory for video calls and meetings.
   - **Solution:** Upgrade to higher resolution cameras (1080p or above) and ensure drivers are updated.

2. **Connectivity Problems**  
   - Users experienced intermittent connectivity with the webcam, leading to disruption during usage.
   - **Solution:** Introduce quality checks for internet connection and ensure stable drivers.

3. **Privacy Concerns**  
   - There have been concerns regarding privacy and data security when using the webcam.
   - **Solution:** Implement a software update to allow users to disable the webcam easily and ensure adherence to data protection regulations.

### Proposed Solutions
- **Upgrade Hardware**  
   Invest in better webcam options focusing on clarity and user feedback for improvement.

- **Regular Software Updates**  
   Ensure that webcam drivers and related software are updated periodically to prevent issues.

- **User Training**  
   Conduct training sessions for users to familiarize them with the features and settings of the webcam software.  

### Monitoring and Follow-Up
- **User Feedback Mechanism**  
   Implement a system for users to easily report issues and provide feedback on the webcam functionality and quality.
- **Performance Review**  
   Schedule follow-up reviews every quarter to assess the effectiveness of the implemented solutions and make further adjustments as necessary.
